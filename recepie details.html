vanila Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all -purpose flour
  • 1/2 sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
    • (softened)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp baking powder

    • Instructions

  • Preheat the oven to 350* F (175*)
  • Cream together the buttre and sugar.
  • Gradually add the flour, milk,and baking powder.
  • pour the batter into a greased pan.
  • Bake for 25-30 minutes.

    Mozeralla Cheese Pizza

    Ingredients

  • I pizza dough
    • (store bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup mozeralla cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cuo tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cuo olives
    • (optional)
  • 1/4 cup bell pepper,choppred (optional)
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

    • Instructions

  • spread the tomato sauce evenly on the dough.
  • sprinkle the shredded mozeralla cheese on the top.
  • Drizzle olive oil over the pizza and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  • bake for 10-12 minutes or untill the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
